Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our catering services

How far in advance should I book my event?

We recommend booking at least 4-6 weeks in advance for smaller events and 2-3 months for larger events like weddings. However, we understand that sometimes events come up quickly, so please contact us even if your event is sooner - we'll do our best to accommodate you!

Do you provide serving staff and equipment?

Yes, we offer full-service catering that includes professional serving staff, appropriate serving equipment, and cleanup. These services can be added to any catering package based on your specific needs and the size of your event.

Can you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Absolutely! We're experienced in accommodating various dietary needs including vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, dairy-free, and specific food allergies. Please let us know about any dietary restrictions when you book your event so we can plan accordingly.

What is your cancellation policy?

We understand that plans can change. For cancellations made more than 14 days before the event, we provide a full refund of any deposit. For cancellations within 7-14 days, we retain 50% of the deposit. Cancellations within 7 days of the event are subject to the full contract amount.

Do you offer tastings before booking?

Yes, we offer complimentary tastings for events with 50+ guests. For smaller events, we charge a modest tasting fee that will be credited toward your final bill if you book with us. Tastings are typically scheduled on Tuesday and Wednesday afternoons.
